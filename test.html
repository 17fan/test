Bird Watching

Several Common Birds:

  • Fratercula
    There is a big mouth, triangular, with a deep groove. The feathers on the back are black, the abdomen is white, the feet are orange red, the face is bright, beautiful and lovely like a parrot, so people call it a sea parrot.There are webbed membranes between the anterior toes and absence of the posterior toes. Wings narrow and short, tail short.
  • Gulls
    Gulls (often informally called seagulls) are birds in the family Laridae. They are most closely related to the terns (family Sternidae) ,Gulls are typically medium to large birds, usually grey or white, often with black markings on the head or wings. They typically have harsh wailing or squawking calls, stout, longish bills, and webbed feet.
  • Pelecanus
    A generic term for eight species of waterfowl of the genus Pelicans. It is about 150 centimeters long and has short and dense feathers. The feathers are white, pink or light grayish brown. Some species of pelicans are larger, with wings 3 meters wide and can fly long distances at speeds exceeding 40 kilometers per hour.
